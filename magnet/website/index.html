Magnet

The word “Magnet” was first used by the Greeks in early 600 B.C. for describing a mysterious stone that attracted iron objects. According to a Greek legend, a shepherd called Magnes discovered the magnetic stone by accident when his staff was mysteriously attracted to the force of the stone.

Magnet is a company focused on new technologies development to several business segments which, as a magnet, attracts the best talents in engineering, multimedia and design.

Our Team

Projects

Team A

Company: Fundação Casa da Musica

Project: Guided Tour to the Casa da Musica

Description: This mobile app aims to give its users a new way to visit "Casa da Música", through a set of 360º photos and movies. This new way to interact with the space will provide a wide range of details and facts that one could not have in a real-time tour and show some of the multiple environments that it can deliver to the users. The app will have a support website to provide the admins the possibility to add new rooms and edit content.

Team B

Company: Adclick

Project: BizMe - Professional event app

Description: An app for communication in the business context, made to simplify and quicken the exchange of contact information, while also offering information about professional events, such as contacts who are attending, and "discover" features.

Team C

Company: iTGrow

Project: iTBirthday

Description: iTBirthday is a web app that aims to enhance the birthday celebration channels within iTGrow, by automatically sending birthday messages via email, sms and the company's facebook. The personalization of the messages allows for a greater impact for each employee and deepens their relationship with the company. The app should also give the admin some statistics of its employees database.

Team D

Company: Tlantic

Project: Intelligent Sales Guide

Description: The goal of the "Intelligent Sales Guide" project is to develop an intelligent sales system that helps the salesmen in asking effective questions to its clients, in order to better recommend appropriate products from a store. This system selects sequences of questions to send to the salesmen based on the specificity of each question and on the effectiveness of similar sequences in the past in selling one or more recommended products.
